The Modern Periodic Law


The Physical and Chemical properties of the elements are a periodic function of their Atomic Numbers

Description


Dimitri Mendeleev, a Russian Chemist arranged the elements in the order of their increasing Atomic Weights as Atomic Number was not found at that time. So he stated his law as "The physical and chemical properties of the elements are a periodic function of their Atomic Weights".
Later when Atomic Number was discovered it was found that the elements were arranged in a correct order whose atomic numbers were their serial numbers. So his law was changed as the above given one.
